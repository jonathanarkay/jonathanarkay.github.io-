Hi! I'm Jonathan.

I'm a playwright. I've also acted, and might do that again one day. But I mainly write because it's easier to hide behind the page.

I'm currently based in NYC, and I grew up on Long Island.

Right now, I'm finishing my MFA in Dramatic Writing at NYU Tisch.

I'm scared of putting personal things on the internet so if you want to talk more email me or find me on Instagram and DM me. LinkedIn works too. I'm not picky. And I answer quick.
